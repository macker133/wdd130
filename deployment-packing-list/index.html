Getting Started

Preparing for an extended underway on an aircraft carrier can be intimidating. There are no Walmarts in the middle of the ocean to visit in case you forget something. Whether you’re a brand-new junior enlisted sailor, a lucky senior enlisted sailor who’s avoided sea duty, or a family member attending a tiger cruise, this website will provide a comprehensive packing guide to prepare you for life at sea aboard one of the world’s most powerful warships.
